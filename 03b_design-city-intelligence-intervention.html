4

Define your first intelligent move

The smallest intervention that shifts the whole pattern.

Leverage, not scale. A First Intelligent Move is not the biggest possible action. It is the most precisely placed small action — the one that nudges the system toward a new pattern rather than reinforcing the existing one.

What makes a move "intelligent"?

  • It targets the feedback loop, not just the visible symptom
  • It is small enough to implement quickly, but precise enough to matter
  • It creates measurable change in at least one system dynamic
  • It opens space for a larger second move — it is the first step of a sequence
  • It considers whose experience it changes first
