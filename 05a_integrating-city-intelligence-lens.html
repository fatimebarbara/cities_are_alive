📖 Integration Guide

Integrating the
City Intelligence Lens

How to embed the frameworks, postures, and tools from this course into your daily work — and make them stick.

The hardest part of any learning journey is what happens after it ends. This guide shows you how to carry the City Intelligence lens into the work you already do — not as extra effort, but as a new way of seeing what was always there.

⚙️Integrate: Optimiser habits
  • Before every budget decision, ask: what does the data say is actually changing?
  • Set at least one measurable metric for each major policy intervention
  • Build a quarterly review habit: are our metrics tracking the right things?
🌉Integrate: Translator habits
  • In every policy brief, include a section: "who is missing from this analysis?"
  • Designate one cross-departmental meeting per quarter to share data that doesn't normally travel
  • Rewrite one technical document per month into community language

Daily integration habits

Check off the habits you commit to embedding in your practice. These are the actions that make the lens stick.

✓
Before each meeting: ask what pattern the agenda item is part of
Not "what is the problem?" but "what is producing this situation — and what would it look like to intervene in the pattern rather than the symptom?"
✓
Weekly: identify one weak signal you noticed but haven't formally processed
A conversation, a behaviour, a change in tone. Write it down. Ask: what pattern might this be part of?
✓
Monthly: use the CII to audit one system or challenge you're working on
Which pillar is most constrained? Where is the interdependence that others are ignoring? What would a First Intelligent Move look like?
✓
In decisions: always ask whose experience is absent from the room
The Translator posture as a permanent habit. Before finalising any decision, name the missing perspectives — and either incorporate them or consciously document why you couldn't.
✓
Before big decisions: trace the path dependency
What does this decision make permanently harder or permanently easier? In 10 years, will you wish you had kept options open here?
✓
Switch posture consciously — notice which one you're in
Once a day, check: am I in Optimiser, Translator, Steward, or Systems Listener mode right now? Is that the mode this situation actually needs?
